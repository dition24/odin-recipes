Spaghetti

Description

This recipe has been handed down from my mother. It is a family favorite and will not be replaced! (Definite husband pleaser!) Serve over any variety of hot cooked pasta.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Tomato sauce
  • Oregano
  • Basil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  1. Cook ground beef until brown. Add in onions and garlic
  2. Stir in tomatoes, tomato sauce, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Serve over cooked spaghetti pasta.
