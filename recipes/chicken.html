Fried Chicken

Description

I experimented with lots of recipes over the years to find the best crispy fried chicken. This is it! It's bits and pieces of hints I have heard over the years. It's more a matter of preparation and cooking method!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Buttermilk
  • Flour
  • Paprika
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Vegetable oil
  1. Mix flour, paprika, salt, and pepper in a plastic bag.
  2. Dip chicken into buttermilk and place into flour mix bag and shake until coated well.
  3. Place chicken on tray and let sit.
  4. Put chicken in hot oil and cook until browned and cooked thoroughly.
