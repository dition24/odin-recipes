Lomo Saltado

Description

This lomo saltado recipe is a traditional and very easy dish to make. I might add that it's also very popular with finicky kids and adults.

Ingredients

  • Beef tri tip
  • French fries
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Yellow chili pepper
  • Vinegar
  • Soy sauce
  • Cilantro
  1. Cook french fries and set to the side for later.
  2. Stir fry beef tri tip in vegetable oil until browned. Add in onion, yellow chili pepper, vingar, and soy sauce.
  3. Once it's all incorportated, add tomato and cilantro and cook for a couple more minutes. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Plate over french fries with rice on the side
